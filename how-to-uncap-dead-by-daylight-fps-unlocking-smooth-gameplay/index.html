What is FPS?

Frames per second, or FPS, is a critical aspect of gaming performance that determines how many frames are displayed on the screen each second. Higher FPS results in smoother and more responsive gameplay, providing a competitive edge to players.

The Role of FPS in Gaming Performance

FPS directly affects the fluidity of in-game movement, aiming precision, and overall responsiveness. Low FPS can lead to stuttering, lag, and delayed inputs, impacting the overall gaming experience.

Why Higher FPS Matters in Dead by Daylight

In Dead by Daylight, where split-second decisions can determine victory or defeat, having a higher FPS can mean the difference between escaping the killer’s grasp or falling prey to their deadly pursuits. Unlocking FPS in Dead by Daylight is crucial for survivors and killers alike.

Checking the Current FPS Cap in Dead by Daylight

Common Performance Issues Caused by FPS Cap
