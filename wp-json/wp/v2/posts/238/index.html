    Prepare for Projection<\/h3>\n

    Before you dive into the world of screen mirroring, there are a few essential steps to ensure a smooth experience. First, you’ll want to check if your Sony TV is compatible with screen mirroring from iPhones. Not all models support this feature, so it’s crucial to verify compatibility. Next, make sure that both your iPhone and Sony TV are running the latest software versions. Keeping your devices updated will prevent compatibility issues and enhance performance during projection. Lastly, for seamless projection, connect both your iPhone and Sony TV to the same Wi-Fi network. This step is vital as screen mirroring requires a stable and reliable connection between the devices.<\/p>\n

    Screen Mirroring Methods<\/h3>\n
