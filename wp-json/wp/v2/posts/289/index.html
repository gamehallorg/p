Introduction<\/h2>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Welcome to the mesmerizing world of Assassin’s Creed Origins, where the sands of time carry you back to ancient Egypt. In this grand adventure, you will follow the footsteps of Bayek, the last of the Medjay warriors, as he embarks on a quest for justice and vengeance. Get ready to uncover the secrets of this rich historical setting and experience the birth of the Assassin Brotherhood.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

    The Siwa Nomad<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    Our journey begins in the serene oasis of Siwa, where Bayek, a devoted protector of the people, faces the tragic loss of his son. Determined to avenge his death, he sets off on a path that will shape the destiny of Egypt. As you explore Siwa’s labyrinthine alleys and vast deserts, you’ll discover the game’s open-world beauty and encounter your first taste of Bayek’s fierce combat skills.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Aya and the Hidden Ones<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    Bayek’s quest takes an unexpected turn as he crosses paths with Cleopatra and becomes entangled in the political web of the ancient world. Alongside his wife Aya, he lays the foundation of the Hidden Ones, the shadowy organization that will evolve into the Assassin Brotherhood. Venture through bustling cities and treacherous tombs, witnessing Bayek’s transformation from a grieving warrior to the formidable Medjay of Egypt.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    The Curse of the Pharaohs<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    In a daring expansion, the Curse of the Pharaohs immerses you in a mystical journey filled with otherworldly foes and enigmatic pharaohs. Bayek must confront the undead and explore the afterlife itself, taking on legendary battles that blur the lines between history and myth. Experience the scale of Egyptian mythology as you face the wrath of ancient gods and uncover the mysteries of the afterlife.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Side Quests and Activities: The Vast World of Ancient Egypt<\/h2>\n

    <\/strong>
    \n<\/p>\n

    Side Quests with Impact<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    Ancient Egypt is teeming with life, and its people seek Bayek’s aid in various side quests. Embrace the role of a Medjay for hire, solving local conflicts and earning the gratitude of the citizens. Delve into Papyri Puzzles to unlock hidden treasures scattered throughout the land. And beware the Roman occupiers who threaten to bring chaos to Egypt’s tranquil lands.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Exploration and Discovery<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    From the grandeur of majestic tombs to the untamed beauty of wildlife, Egypt invites you to explore its every corner. Unravel the riddles of tombs and temples, uncovering hidden secrets and historical wonders. Engage in hunting and taming, mastering the art of survival in the wilderness. And embark on breathtaking voyages along the Nile, unveiling the stories hidden within ancient waters.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Combat and Skill Progression<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    As Bayek, you’ll have a vast array of weapons at your disposal. Craft and upgrade your arsenal to suit your playstyle. Master the art of melee and ranged combat, employing a wide range of techniques to defeat foes. Customize Bayek’s abilities through skill trees, developing a warrior tailored to your preferences and tactics.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Time Factors: How Long Does it Take to Complete Assassin’s Creed Origins?<\/h2>\n

    <\/strong>
    \n<\/p>\n

    Main Story Duration<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    The average playtime to complete the core campaign of Assassin’s Creed Origins is around 30-40 hours. However, the actual duration varies based on your exploration and side quest involvement. The story-driven choices you make also impact your progress, influencing the game’s outcome and adding to its replay value. If you’re an experienced player or a speedrunner, you might complete the main story in a shorter time.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Completionist Playthrough<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    If you’re one to savor every detail and embrace a completionist playstyle, expect the journey to extend significantly. Collectibles, achievements, and side quests will enrich your experience and bring you closer to 100% completion. Embrace the joy of fully immersing yourself in the vast world of ancient Egypt.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Factors Affecting Playtime<\/h3>\n

    <\/strong><\/p>\n

    Various factors can influence how long your adventure will take. Your gaming style, whether casual or hardcore, will impact the pace of progress. Choosing a higher difficulty level may pose greater challenges and extend playtime. Additionally, the inclusion of extra content such as downloadable expansions can expand your journey even further.<\/p>\n
