    What is Symmetry in Aseprite?<\/h3>\n

    Symmetry in pixel art refers to the ability to create mirrored or repeated patterns on one side of the canvas, which are automatically replicated on the other side. It’s like having a virtual kaleidoscope for your artwork. This feature comes in handy when you want to create symmetrical characters, objects, or backgrounds.<\/p>\n

    Accessing Symmetry Tools in Aseprite<\/h3>\n

    Activating the symmetry tool in Aseprite is a breeze. You can find it in the toolbar, and with a single click, you’ll unlock a world of creative possibilities. Once enabled, you can choose from various symmetry options, such as horizontal, vertical, and even radial symmetry. The best part is that you can customize the settings to match the specific needs of your artwork.<\/p>\n
